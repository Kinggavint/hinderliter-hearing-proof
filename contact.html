Insurance and payment questions

Do you accept Medicare for hearing tests?

Medicare coverage can vary by plan and the type of appointment. If you call our office, we can help you understand what to expect before you come in.

Do you accept Blue Cross Blue Shield of Michigan?

Coverage depends on your specific BCBS plan. Call our office and we will help you verify benefits and next steps.

Do you offer financing for hearing aids?

We can discuss payment options during your visit and help you choose a plan that fits your needs.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring a photo ID, your insurance card (if applicable), and any recent hearing test results or medical notes you have. If you currently wear hearing aids, bring them and any accessories.

How long does a hearing evaluation take?

Many appointments take about an hour, but timing can vary depending on your needs. When you schedule, we can confirm what to expect.

Can I get a same-week appointment?

Same-week appointments may be available depending on the schedule. Call the office and we will do our best to find a time that works.
