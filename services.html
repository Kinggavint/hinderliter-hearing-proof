What We Offer

Our Hearing Services

From comprehensive evaluations to the latest hearing aid technology, we offer a full range of audiological services tailored to your needs.

Diagnostic Testing

Comprehensive Hearing Evaluations

Our thorough hearing evaluations go beyond a simple hearing test. We use advanced audiometric testing to build a complete picture of your hearing health, identifying the type and degree of any hearing loss.

Understanding your unique hearing profile is the essential first step toward better hearing. Our evaluations are conducted in a comfortable, unhurried environment so we can give you and your hearing the attention they deserve.

  • Pure tone and speech audiometry
  • Tympanometry and middle ear analysis
  • Otoacoustic emissions testing
  • Detailed results review and recommendations

Advanced Technology

Hearing Aid Solutions

We work with the leading hearing aid manufacturers to provide you with the best technology available. But choosing a hearing aid isn't just about technology — it's about finding the right fit for your lifestyle.

Our experts take the time to understand how you communicate, where you spend your time, and what matters most to you. Then we fit, program, and verify your hearing aids with precision to ensure optimal performance.

  • Access to all major hearing aid brands
  • Custom fitting and real-ear verification
  • Programming tailored to your lifestyle
  • Free assistive device with hearing aid purchase (some restrictions apply)
  • Ongoing adjustments and support

Relief & Management

Tinnitus Management

Tinnitus — ringing, buzzing, or humming in the ears — can significantly impact your quality of life. Our audiologists are experienced in evaluating and managing tinnitus using evidence-based approaches.

We work with you to identify potential causes and develop a personalized management plan that may include sound therapy, hearing aids with tinnitus features, counseling, and lifestyle modifications.

  • Comprehensive tinnitus evaluation
  • Sound therapy and masking options
  • Hearing aids with tinnitus management features
  • Personalized coping strategies

Custom Ear Molds

A proper fit is essential for hearing aid comfort and performance. Our custom ear molds are precision-crafted to match the unique contours of your ear, providing superior comfort for all-day wear.

  • Custom hearing aid ear molds
  • Hearing protection molds
  • Swim molds and musician molds
  • Precise impression techniques
