Our Story

About Hinderliter Hearing Services

For over 18 years, we've been helping Birmingham and the surrounding communities hear life more clearly — with warmth, expertise, and genuine care.

A Personal Commitment to Better Hearing

Dr. Kristin Hinderliter was born with hearing loss. That personal experience shaped a lifelong commitment to helping others hear the world more fully. She understands, on a deeply personal level, the impact that hearing loss can have on relationships, confidence, and quality of life.

After earning her Doctorate of Audiology from Salus University and spending years providing hearing health care in the Birmingham area, Dr. Hinderliter founded Hinderliter Hearing Services with a clear philosophy: communication is fundamental to a fulfilled life.

Today, our practice is home to a team of seven dedicated professionals, each committed to delivering personalized care in a warm, unhurried environment. We believe every patient deserves to be truly heard — and to hear life at its best.

Our Team

The People Behind Your Care

Our team brings together decades of combined experience in audiology, hearing instrument technology, and patient care.

KH

Kristin Hinderliter, Au.D., FAAA

Owner & Doctor of Audiology

Born with hearing loss, Dr. Hinderliter brings a deeply personal understanding to her practice. With over 18 years of experience providing hearing health care in Birmingham, MI, she is a trusted name in audiology.

Raised in Birmingham, she attended Seaholm High School before earning her BA in Audiology & Speech Sciences and MA in Audiology from Michigan State University. She completed her Doctorate from Salus University in 2005 and previously co-owned a private practice. Her guiding philosophy: communication is fundamental to a fulfilled life.

LL

Laurie Long, M.A., FAAA

Audiologist

Laurie joined the practice in 2019, bringing 23 years of audiology experience. She spent over 15 years working alongside ENT physicians, where she started their hearing aid program.

She holds a Master's degree from Western Michigan University (1996) and a BA from Central Michigan University (1993). Laurie is certified by ASHA, a Fellow of the American Academy of Audiology, and licensed in the state of Michigan.

SS

Sean Sorrow, HIS

Hearing Instrument Specialist

Sean is a licensed Hearing Instrument Specialist known for exceptional diagnostic testing experience. Trained in December 2010 and licensed in the state of Michigan, he is a member of the International Hearing Society.

With more than 10 years of community service, Sean focuses on personalized programming and verification to ensure every patient gets the best possible hearing experience from their devices.
